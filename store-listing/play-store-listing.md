## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

**Myraba** is Nigeria's community-first fintech app — built around the age-old African tradition of thrift saving (ajo/esusu), reimagined for the smartphone generation.

---

### 💸 Send & Receive Money Instantly
Transfer funds to anyone using their MyrabaTag (@handle), 10-digit account number, or by scanning their QR code. Fast, secure, and zero friction.

### 🏦 Thrift Savings — The Nigerian Way
Join community thrift groups (ajo/esusu) and save consistently with friends, family, or strangers. Myraba automates contributions, tracks your position, and pays out your turn — digitally and transparently.

**Public Thrift Groups** — Join an existing group based on amount and frequency that fits your budget.
**Private Thrift Groups** — Create a group with people you trust, set your own rules, and manage payouts your way.

### 🎁 Send Gifts That Mean Something
Browse curated gift items and send them directly to a friend's Myraba account. Whether it's a birthday, celebration, or just because — gifting is seamless and personal.

### 📱 Pay All Your Bills in One Place
- Airtime & Data (MTN, Airtel, Glo, 9mobile)
- Electricity (all DISCOs)
- Cable TV (DSTV, GOtv, Startimes)
- Betting account top-ups
- And more

### 📊 Financial Pulse
Get a clear picture of where your money goes. Our holographic spending breakdown shows you exactly how much went to transfers, bills, thrift, gifts — by category, by month, beautifully visualised.

### 🔐 Built for Security
- JWT-secured sessions
- OTP verification for every new account
- Multi-factor authentication (TOTP) support
- KYC identity verification
- End-to-end encrypted sensitive data

### 🌍 MyrabaTag — Your Money Identity
Every user gets a unique MyrabaTag (like a username for your wallet). Share it anywhere, receive money instantly — no account numbers to memorise.
